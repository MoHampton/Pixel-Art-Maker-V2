Pixel Art Maker Instructions
  • Grid Size: Choose the canvas grid size for your new creation. (Default: 15 x 15)

  • Pick A Color: Go with the Midas touch. (Default: Gold) Or pick a new color.

  • Draw:
    • Paint: Paint a pixel with a single-click. Make a paint stroke by pressing the mouse down and dragging.
      Careful: the stroke will continue until the mouse is clicked again.
    • Erase: Everyone makes mistakes. Double-click the pixel to remove colors.
